Spokane Floormart

Spokane Floormart is a flooring solution center for professional installation of corporate and consumer flooring. Floormart was opened in 1987 by Wayne Rome, and passed onto Mike Rome in 1999.

Our Work here in Spokane

Spokane Vetran's Arena

Floormart laid the entire floorplan for the Spokane Veterans Memorial Arena in 1993. The floorplan is an expanse of over 32,000 square feet.

Jimmy Johns

All flooring for Jimmy Johns locations in Spokane is laid by floormart.

Buffalo Wild Wings

The new Buffalo Wild Wings bar's floor was laid by floormart.

Primary Services

Commercial Installation

Construction of commercial businesses is a stressful and expensive challenge to undertake. Floormart has laid flooring for over 40 local Spokane businesses.

Consumer Home Flooring

Just because Floormart is known for commercial installation does not mean consumer flooring isn't welcomed. Floormart began as a consumer flooring solution and remains one to this day.

Products

Carpet

Carpet is a popular consumer purchase. Floormart provides brands from Shaw carpeting and Mohawk.

Ceramic Tile

Ceramic Tile is popular in both consumer and commercial purchases. Possible applications could be in kitchens, bathrooms and walls.

Luxury Vinyl Plank

Luxury Vinyl Plank is a consumer flooring solution and is less expensive and more customizable than hardwood.

Hardwood

Hardwood is timelessly beautiful and is a very popular modern flooring solution. Hardwood look, style and expense varies on the availability of the wood species.

Linoleum

Linoleum Is the trusted commercial solution. Durable and intended to last and survive the beating of high volume footraffic.

Epoxy Industrial Floors

High performance flooring for specialized commercial application.
